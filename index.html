FAQ

Common questions

Is LexSumm a replacement for a lawyer? +
No. LexSumm is an AI tool to help you understand legal documents faster. For legal advice specific to your situation, always consult a qualified attorney. We help you arrive at that conversation better informed.
What types of documents can I summarize? +
Contracts, NDAs, leases, employment agreements, court filings, privacy policies, terms of service, partnership agreements, settlement agreements, corporate bylaws, and more. If it's a legal document, LexSumm can handle it.
Is my document kept private? +
Absolutely. We process your document and immediately discard it. We use encryption in transit and at rest, and we have a strict zero-retention policy. Your confidential documents stay confidential.
How accurate is the AI summarization? +
LexSumm achieves 97%+ accuracy on standard legal document types. It's trained specifically on legal language. We recommend reviewing critical clauses manually for high-stakes decisions.
What file formats are supported? +
PDF, DOCX, DOC, and plain text input are all supported. Files up to 50MB can be processed. Scanned PDFs with OCR text are also supported.
Is there a free tier? +
Yes! LexSumm is completely free to use. You can summarize documents without creating an account. Advanced features and bulk processing will be available in premium plans.
